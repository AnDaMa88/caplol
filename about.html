    Craig Yoe is a dynamic illustrator and author whose creative journey spans decades—from his early days as creative director for the Muppets, Disney, and Nickelodeon to his award-winning work in children's publishing. Today, he channels his playful spirit into delightful kids’ books that spark laughter and inspire a lifelong love of reading. With a signature style that’s both engaging and imaginative, Craig’s work—such as his popular Squeaky Clean Super Funny series and Captain LOL adventures—brings humor and heart to young readers everywhere.
